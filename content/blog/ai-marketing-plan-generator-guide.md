# AI Marketing Plan Generator Guide

Marketing plans often fail because they are either too vague or too slow to produce.

## What to include in your prompt

- Goal window (monthly, quarterly)
- Target audience
- Channels and budget assumptions
- KPI targets

## Example prompt

> "Create a quarterly marketing plan for a B2B AI product with SEO, paid search, and webinar channels."
