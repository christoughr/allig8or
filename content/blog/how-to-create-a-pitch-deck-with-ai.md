# How to Create a Pitch Deck with AI in Minutes

Investor meetings do not wait for you to perfect every slide master. If you can describe your company in a paragraph, you can get a **downloadable .pptx** in under a minute with [allig8tor](/app?tool=presentation).

## What makes a good AI prompt for decks?

Be specific about:

- **Audience** — angels, Series A, or enterprise buyers
- **Slide count** — often 10–15 for pitches
- **Story arc** — problem, solution, market, traction, team, ask
- **Tone** — bold startup, conservative enterprise, etc.

## Example prompt

> “12-slide seed pitch for a developer tools startup that sells AI code review. Include problem, solution, why now, business model, and $1.5M raise ask. Clean, minimal design.”

## Why use allig8tor instead of chat-only AI?

Chat gives you bullet outlines. **allig8tor** outputs a real **PowerPoint file** — structured slides you can edit and send tonight.
