# AI Excel Spreadsheet Generator

Spreadsheets are where plans become numbers. Starting from a blank workbook wastes time — especially for **runway models**, **marketing budgets**, and **OKR trackers**.

## When an AI spreadsheet helps

- You need a **first draft** to share with a co-founder or accountant
- You want **labeled columns** and **sample formulas** (SUM, growth %, monthly burn)
- You are not an Excel power user but know what rows you need

## Prompt tips

List the **rows** (categories), **columns** (months or scenarios), and any **totals** you expect.

Example:

> “24-month SaaS financial model: MRR, COGS, opex (payroll, tools, marketing), net burn, cash balance. Start MRR at $8k growing 12% MoM.”
