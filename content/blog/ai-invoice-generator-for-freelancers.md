# AI Invoice Generator for Freelancers

Late invoices and inconsistent formatting delay payments. A repeatable invoice workflow fixes both.

## Minimum invoice structure

- Client and sender details
- Service line items
- Subtotal, tax, total
- Payment terms (for example, net-15)

## Prompt template

> "Create a freelance UX invoice in USD for May work. Include 5 line items, subtotal, 10% tax, total due, and net-15 terms."

## Why teams use AI here

Invoice generation should not consume creative time. AI helps standardize output while keeping each invoice editable.
