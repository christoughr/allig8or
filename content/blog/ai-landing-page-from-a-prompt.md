# Build a Landing Page from a Prompt

Before you hire a designer or wire up Webflow, validate your **headline, offer, and CTA** with a generated page.

## What allig8tor generates

- Hero with headline + subcopy
- Feature grid and social proof placeholders
- CTA section pointing to your signup or waitlist
- **Live preview** in the browser

## Strong prompts include

1. **Product name** and one-line value prop  
2. **Target customer**  
3. **Visual style** — dark/light, accent color, “minimal” vs “bold”  
4. **Sections** you want: hero, features, pricing teaser, footer
