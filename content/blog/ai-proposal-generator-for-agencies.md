# AI Proposal Generator for Agencies and Freelancers

Proposal quality affects close rate, but proposal production is repetitive. Most teams rewrite the same structure every week.

## What AI should handle in proposals

- Scope and deliverables structure
- Timeline and milestone formatting
- Consistent section hierarchy
- Draft-ready language you can quickly edit

## Prompt pattern that works

Include:

1. Client type and project goal
2. Deliverables
3. Timeline
4. Pricing model
5. Tone (formal, concise, strategic)

Example prompt:

> "Create a client proposal for B2B SaaS website redesign. Include 8-week timeline, deliverables by phase, and milestone-based pricing."

## Why this improves ROI

When proposals are faster to produce, teams can ship more outbound offers and spend review time on strategy, not formatting.
