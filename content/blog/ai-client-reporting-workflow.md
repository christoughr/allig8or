# AI Client Reporting Workflow for Agencies

Client reporting often breaks consistency: one month is detailed, the next is rushed. A structured AI workflow keeps quality stable.

## Core sections to include

- Executive summary
- KPI snapshot
- Work completed
- Risks and blockers
- Next-month actions

## Prompt example

> "Generate a monthly marketing client report with KPI summary, campaign wins, blockers, and next-month action plan."

## Why this matters for retention

Consistent reporting quality improves trust. Teams that communicate clearly keep clients longer and justify premium fees.
