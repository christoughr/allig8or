# AI Document Automation ROI for Service Teams

Most teams underestimate document production cost because it is spread across many people and small tasks.

## Simple ROI model

Track:

- Average hours spent per deliverable
- Number of deliverables per month
- Blended hourly team cost

Then compare against a monthly AI production subscription.

## Example

If your team saves 20-40 production hours monthly, premium tooling can be net-positive even at high plan tiers.

## What to automate first

1. Repeating proposals
2. Monthly reports
3. Investor or client decks
4. Spreadsheet templates with recurring structure
