# Prompt-to-PPTX Best Practices

If your AI deck output feels generic, the issue is usually prompt specificity, not just the model.

## What to specify in every deck prompt

- Audience (investors, clients, internal team)
- Slide count
- Tone (formal, bold, analytical)
- Required sections (problem, solution, traction, ask)
- Visual style preference

## Strong prompt example

> "Create a 10-slide investor deck for a fintech startup. Include problem, solution, market, traction, business model, and fundraising ask. Keep copy concise and data-first."

## Fast QA checklist after generation

- Do slides follow a logical narrative?
- Is each slide focused on one idea?
- Are speaker notes present where needed?
